**Privacy Policy for Geo Board Game**
Last updated: April 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Geo Board Game ("we", "us", or "our") handles information from users ("you" or "your") of the Geo Board Game mobile application (the "App").

---

### 1. Information We Collect

We do not require users to create an account or provide personal information.

The App may automatically collect limited information, including:

* **Device identifiers** (such as Advertising ID), used only to display ads.

We do not collect names, emails, or any personally identifiable information.

---

### 2. Use of Ads

The App uses **Google AdMob** to display advertisements.

AdMob may collect and use device identifiers to provide ads. These ads are designed to be **non-intrusive and not disruptive to gameplay**.

You can choose to opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device’s ad settings.

---

### 3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share user data.

Device identifiers may be shared with **Google AdMob** solely for the purpose of delivering advertisements.

---

### 4. Gameplay and Data

Geo Board Game is a simple puzzle game where players draw triangles and progress through levels.

* No gameplay data is stored externally
* No personal data is linked to your progress

---

### 5. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13.
We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

---

### 6. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the limited data used within the App.
However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

---

### 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
